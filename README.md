### Emerging Curative Technologies (Clinical Trials)

- **Bispecific T-cell Engagers (BiTEs)**: Off-the-shelf, no manufacturing delay (ALL, SCLC, Myeloma)
- **Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs)**: Targeted chemo delivery, proven efficacy (Breast, Bladder, Ovarian)
- **Allogeneic CAR-T**: Off-the-shelf, no patient manufacturing (Blood cancers, Solid tumors (research))
- **CAR-NK Cells**: Off-the-shelf, less CRS/neurotoxicity (Blood cancers, Solid tumors)
- **Tumor-Infiltrating Lymphocytes (TIL)**: Polyclonal, targets multiple antigens (Melanoma (approved), NSCLC, cervical (trials))
- **TCR-T Cell Therapy**: Can target intracellular proteins (Synovial sarcoma, Uveal melanoma)
- **KRAS G12D Inhibitors**: Most common KRAS mutation (Pancreatic (30%), Colorectal (10%))
- **Degraders (PROTACs/Molecular Glues)**: Degrades rather than inhibits (Breast, Synovial sarcoma, Various)
- **Radioligand Therapy**: Targeted radiation delivery (Prostate (approved), NETs (approved), Others (trials))
- **In Situ Vaccination/Oncolytic Virus**: Converts cold tumors to hot (Melanoma, Solid tumors)

### Emerging Screening Technologies

- **Multi-Cancer Early Detection (MCED)**: cfDNA methylation patterns - Detects: 50+ cancer types (Sensitivity: 51.5% overall, 90%+ for late stage)
- **Circulating Tumor DNA (ctDNA)**: Tumor mutation detection in blood - Detects: Any with mutations (Sensitivity: 70-90% for advanced)
- **Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs)**: Intact tumor cells in blood - Detects: Breast, Prostate, Colorectal (Sensitivity: 30-70%)
- **Exosome/Extracellular Vesicle Analysis**: Tumor-derived vesicles with RNA/protein - Detects: Prostate, Lung, Pancreas (research) (Sensitivity: 70-80%)
- **Proteomics/Protein Biomarkers**: Multi-protein panels - Detects: Multiple (Sensitivity: Varies by cancer)
- **Breath Analysis (Volatile Organic Compounds)**: VOC patterns from metabolism - Detects: Lung, Colorectal, others (Sensitivity: 70-85%)
- **AI-Enhanced Imaging**: Deep learning on CT/MRI/pathology - Detects: Lung, Breast, Prostate, others (Sensitivity: 90-95%)
- **Methylation Arrays**: Genome-wide methylation - Detects: Brain tumors, many others (Sensitivity: 95%+ for classification)
