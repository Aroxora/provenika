# Brand & domain — recommendation

Researched for a VC/YC-friendly identity: short, coined, ownable, broad (not onco-locked),
and evidence/provenance-evoking — **not** "cure cancer" (an overpromise that reads as naïve to
investors). Availability was **checked, not guessed** (WHOIS + Apple iTunes Search API), in keeping
with the project's compute-or-cite rule.

## Recommendation: **Provenika**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Provenika (from *proven* / *provenance*) — coined, pronounceable, brandable, no incumbent clash |
| **Domain** | **provenika.com — AVAILABLE** (WHOIS: "No match for PROVENIKA.COM", checked 2026-06-14) |
| **iOS app name** | **"Provenika" — clear** (Apple iTunes Search returned no matching software; the 3 fuzzy hits are unrelated apps) |
| **iOS bundle ID** | `com.provenika.app` (reverse-DNS of the domain; no Provenika app exists, so it's free to register in Apple Developer) |
| **Handles** | check @provenika / @provenika_bio on X/LinkedIn/GitHub before launch (not verified here) |
| **Why it fits VC/YC** | coined + ownable (trademark-able), broad enough to scale beyond oncology, signals the moat (provenance) without overclaiming |

## Verified alternates (also available .com)

| Domain | Note |
|--------|------|
| `oncoproof.com` | Descriptive (onco + proof); narrower scope; "proof" slightly overclaims vs. our "triage ≠ validation" stance |
| `attestbio.com` | "Attest" = verify/bear witness — strong fit; but a consumer brand "Attest" (attest.com) exists, mild confusion risk |
| `attestrx.com` | `rx` skews pharma; available |

**Avoid:** `sciteproof.com` (available but clashes with **scite.ai**, a direct evidence-tool competitor).
